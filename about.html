About Us

Our History, Mission & Vision

Our Vision
To achieve and maintain global recognition for seamless hospitality provision that always exceeds customer expectations.

Our Mission
To be the preferred destination of choice, delighting our guests every time by creating engaging experiences through professional, personalized and flexible hospitality services.

Who we are
Sifa Lounge is a modern and comfortable hotel providing a serene and quiet environment offering every guest a true definition of hospitality. The hotel is conveniently located in Muyenga-Bukasa, along Mugalu Road, Kampala, Uganda. It is within a 45 minutes' drive from Entebbe International Airport and only 15 minutes drive from the city center. Our location is perfect and convenient for honeymooners, business travelers, tourists, and those who wish to have a memorable visit to Uganda, the 'Pearl of Africa'.

Our Customers
Guided by the maxim that the guest is the king and centre of our attention, Sifa Lounge, before anything else, promotes hospitality and courtesy towards guests, by giving special attention to details and offering more than is expected.
