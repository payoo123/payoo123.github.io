Our Accommodation

Standard Room (PRICE PER NIGHT: US$ 20)

Enjoy all the luxury and comfort you have come to expect from a Sifa Lounge in the Classic Room. Designed to be as comfortable and as functional as possible, leaving you to focus on the important, professional matters in hand. This lounge room is equipped to support the corporate traveler as well as, thanks to the addition of a working desk and laptop-sized safe, the perfect base away from the office.
Deluxe Room (PRICE PER NIGHT: US$ 30)

Treat yourself to a Deluxe Room for that extra-special touch of space and style. Designed with soothing, yet stylish, shades that form the perfect backdrop for your work or relaxation. Designed in authentic African style and elegantly furnished with upscale furnishings and amenities, our 52-squaremeter Deluxe Rooms offer a quiet sophistication ideal for business or leisure stays. For guests that are looking for a little extra, the deluxe room might be the answer.
Executive Suite (PRICE PER NIGHT: US$ 40)

Designed as the perfect sanctuary from the stresses and demands of business life, the Executive Suite offers a truly distinctive level of comfort and elegance. Take a well-deserved break in this beautifully appointed room and enjoy executive benefits.
